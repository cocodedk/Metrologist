Actual size. Ready to use.

Print this page at 100% / Actual size to get a physical red-white reference stick exactly 20.0 cm × 4.0 cm (7.87 in × 1.57 in) — the default dimensions you enter in the Metrologist app's stick settings.

How to use

  • 01 Print at 100% / Actual size. In your print dialog, disable "Fit to page", "Shrink to fit", or "Scale to fit". Set scale to 100%. This is critical — any scaling makes the stick the wrong physical size.
  • 02 Verify with a ruler. The calibration ruler printed below the stick must measure exactly 10 cm (100 mm) with a real ruler. If it doesn't, reprint with corrected scale settings.
  • 03 Cut it out and mount it. Cut along the stick outline. Glue or tape it to something flat and rigid — cardboard, a ruler, a thin board. The stick must stay flat and straight when photographed.
  • 04 Enter dimensions in the app. In Metrologist settings → Reference stick, enter Length: 20.0 cm and Width: 4.0 cm (or 7.87 in × 1.57 in).
  • 05 Photograph and measure. Place the stick on the surface, take a photo, mark the four corners and two stick ends — done.
